How to Play

☝️

Draw

Point your index finger up and move your hand to draw glowing lines.

✌️

Hover

Hold up two fingers to move your cursor around without drawing.

🖐️

Erase

Hold up three fingers to activate the eraser and delete lines.

🎨

Colors

Tap the color dots on the left side to change your drawing color.
